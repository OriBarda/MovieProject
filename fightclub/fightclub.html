F**** C ***

F**** C *** is a 1999 film directed by David Fincher and based on the 1996 novel of the same name by Chuck Palahniuk. The film stars Edward Norton, Brad Pitt, and Helena Bonham Carter. It explores themes of consumerism, masculinity, and identity crisis. The unnamed protagonist (played by Norton) forms an underground fight club as a way to escape his mundane life. As the club grows, it takes on a more sinister direction, leading to unexpected consequences.

Main Actors:

  • Edward Norton as The Narrator
  • Brad Pitt as Tyler Durden
  • Helena Bonham Carter as Marla Singer
